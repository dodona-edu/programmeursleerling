Print the multiplication table for a given factor.

### Input

An integer $$n \in \mathbb{Z}$$.

### Output

The multiplication table of $$n$$ from 1 to 10.

### Example

#### Input:

```
12
```

#### Output:

```
1 * 12 = 12
2 * 12 = 24
3 * 12 = 36
4 * 12 = 48
5 * 12 = 60
6 * 12 = 72
7 * 12 = 84
8 * 12 = 96
9 * 12 = 108
10 * 12 = 120
```
